Welcome to Behavior Analysis!

Behavior analysis is a cloud based survey designed to asses individuals mental health status.
Aim of this survey is to give user an accurate and reliable information on their mental health status based on behavioral measures and symptoms which can identify mental health disorders, and classify whether users have normal mental health condition, or whether they belong to any category of mental health disorder such as anxiety, depression, loneliness, or stress and finally suggest further steps needed to be taken.
